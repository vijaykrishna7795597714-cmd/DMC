About Divasa Fresh

Who We Are

Divasa Fresh was created to bring structure, trust, and reliability to fresh produce sourcing by working directly with farmers and delivering quality-assured fruits and vegetables through an organized supply chain.

What We Do

  • Develop structured sourcing partnerships with farmers to ensure consistent and reliable supply
  • Aggregate fresh produce through planned procurement and centralized handling
  • Apply standardized quality, grading, and consistency checks aligned with market requirements
  • Serve households, retail chains, hotels, restaurants, and institutions with dependable farm-fresh supply

Why Divasa Fresh Exists

Divasa Fresh exists to bring structure, reliability, and transparency to the farm-to-market supply chain. By taking ownership of sourcing, aggregation, quality control, and distribution, we bridge the gap between farms and end consumers, creating a balanced ecosystem where farmers are valued partners, operations are efficient, and customers receive quality-assured produce they can trust.

Upcoming Launch: Solar-Dried Health Powders

At Divasa Fresh, we follow a natural and careful solar drying method to preserve the true essence of farm-fresh produce. Fresh leaves and roots are sourced directly from trusted farmers and cleaned thoroughly.

They are then solar dried at controlled, low temperatures, allowing moisture to reduce naturally without harsh heat exposure. This helps retain natural color, aroma, and essential nutrients while avoiding chemicals or artificial drying methods.

Careful Processing & Packing

Once solar dried, the produce is finely ground in hygienic facilities under strict quality control. The powders are immediately packed in food-grade, moisture-resistant packaging to protect freshness and purity.

Each pack is handled carefully to prevent contamination and nutrient loss, ensuring consistent quality from processing to delivery.

Farmers Are Our Partners

Built on Collaboration

At Divasa Fresh, we believe that long-term success in food supply begins with strong farmer partnerships. We work closely with farmers at the source, planning harvests, aligning quality expectations, and coordinating supply in advance. This collaborative approach provides farmers with organized market access and operational support, allowing them to focus on cultivation with greater confidence.

A Structured Partnership Model

Divasa Fresh takes responsibility for aggregation, quality checks, and distribution through a structured sourcing model designed to simplify the supply chain. By coordinating procurement at the farm level and managing aggregation and distribution centrally, we reduce unnecessary handling and operational inefficiencies. This partnership-driven model helps farmers realize better value for their produce, while ensuring consumers receive fresh, quality-assured vegetables delivered with transparency, efficiency, and fair pricing.
